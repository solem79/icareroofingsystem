About Us

About iCARE Roofing System

At iCARE Roofing System, we provide professional roofing services and the supply, installation, and maintenance of high-quality roofing materials for residential, commercial, and industrial projects across Ghana.

We specialize in stone-coated roofing tiles, aluminium and long-span roofing sheets, gutters, and complete roofing accessories. From new roof installations to roof repairs and replacements, our team delivers durable, weather-resistant, and aesthetically pleasing roofing solutions tailored to each client’s needs.

Our Story

From a modest beginning to becoming a trusted name in professional roofing services and stone-coated roofing systems in Ghana, iCARE Roofing System has remained committed to quality workmanship, durable materials, and total client satisfaction.

2017

iCARE Roofing System was established with a clear focus on providing reliable roofing installation and repair services using aluminium and stone-coated roofing sheets for residential buildings in Accra.

2019

Expanded into full stone-coated roofing systems, gutters, and modern roofing accessories, serving homeowners, contractors, and developers across Accra and surrounding regions.

2022

Successfully completed hundreds of roofing projects, strengthening our reputation for durable roofing solutions, neat installations, and dependable service delivery across Ghana.

2026

Recognized as a trusted roofing company in Ghana, known for stone-coated roofing expertise, premium materials, and timely project execution for residential, commercial, and industrial clients.

ABOUT

iCARE Roofing System is a trusted roofing service provider in Ghana, delivering reliable roof installations, repairs, maintenance, and protective roofing solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial buildings. We are committed to quality workmanship, safety, and long-lasting results.
