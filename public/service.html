Our Services

iCARE Roofing System provides high-quality roofing materials, professional roof installation, and reliable roofing solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects across Ghana.

Roofing Materials Supply

We supply premium-quality roofing materials including stone-coated roofing tiles, aluminium roofing sheets, long-span sheets, gutters, and roofing accessories — sourced for durability, beauty, and long-lasting performance.

Roof Installation Services

Our experienced team provides professional installation of stone-coated tiles, aluminium roofing sheets, and long-span roofing for homes, offices, churches, and commercial buildings.

Roof Repairs & Replacement

We handle roof repairs, roof replacement, and maintenance services to fix leaks, damaged sheets, rusted roofs, and aging roofing systems.

Stone-Coated Roofing Solutions

We specialize in stone-coated roofing systems that combine modern beauty with strength, sound insulation, and superior weather resistance.

Residential & Commercial Roofing

From private homes to office complexes and estates, we deliver reliable roofing solutions tailored to your building type and budget.

Contractor & Bulk Supply Services

We work with contractors, developers, and property owners, offering bulk supply, competitive pricing, and expert roofing material advice for large-scale projects.
